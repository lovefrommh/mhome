WHY MAMRING HOMESTAY?

A small homestay located in the womb of Kurseong forest hides many eco system, culture and beauty. A journey of adventure and wonderful serene have mesmerised many visitor since last 2 years of our opening. Our words are yet so small to compare it with the guest review and feedback we have received. Our friendly neighbours have helped us alot to develop the site and build one of the best tourist place with small waterfall, cozy place and silent nature. Many tourist have experinced a different experince of exotic visit, a real escape to something beautiful and have self-healed alot.
When it comes to health and beauty - what's more pure than the organic fruits and vegetables? the locals have mastered in the field of organic vegetables and are supplied to most of the big towns like Gangtok, Siliguri, Darjeeling, Kurseong and Mirik. We have also mastered in the field of dairy products, pisciculture, goatery and broiler farming. The farmers have been instructed and trained with hand-on for organic manufaturing and production and the impacts of chemical use to human health and soil itself. When it comes to food, you can be rest assured.
